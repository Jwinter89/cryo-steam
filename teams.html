WHO IT'S FOR

Three Use Cases, One Platform

⚙

OPERATOR TRAINING

Onboard new hires on DCS operations before they touch a live board. Practice upset response, alarm management, and normal operations in a safe environment.

🎓

ENGINEERING EDUCATION

Give students hands-on experience with real process control. Interactive P&ID diagrams, PID tuning, cascade control, and NGL recovery — not just textbook theory.

Run crisis scenarios for annual competency testing. Compressor trips, H₂S releases, freeze-offs, column floods — measure response time and decision quality.
